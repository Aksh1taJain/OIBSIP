Live Flame Baking

Our oak-wood ovens hit 900 °F — the only way to get that signature leopard-spotted char and airy, blistered crust Napoli is famous for.

48-Hour Dough

Our dough ferments slowly for two days using only Caputo 00 flour, spring water, sea salt, and a pinch of yeast — nothing more, nothing less.

DOP Ingredients

Every sauce starts with hand-crushed San Marzano DOP tomatoes. Our fior di latte arrives fresh three times a week from Campania.
